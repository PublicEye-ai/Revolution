## üîç Phase 2: Pattern Recognition

### Success Patterns to Identify:
1. **Honest Communication**
   - Acknowledging difficulties
   - Explaining trade-offs
   - Showing progress
   - Maintaining vision

2. **Quick Wins**
   - Visible improvements
   - Symbolic victories
   - Tangible benefits
   - Participation opportunities

3. **Adaptive Messaging**
   - Evolving narrative
   - Learning acknowledgment
   - Course corrections
   - Hope maintenance

### Failure Patterns to Avoid:
1. **Over-Promising**
   - Utopian visions
   - Impossible timelines
   - Magic solutions
   - Denial of trade-offs

2. **Under-Delivery**
   - No visible progress
   - Elite benefits only
   - Broken commitments
   - Excuse making

3. **Communication Breakdown**
   - Silence on failures
   - Blame shifting
   - Gaslighting
   - Disconnect from reality
